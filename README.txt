## 🔐 Tips Learned

- Use **at least 12+ characters**
- Include **uppercase, lowercase, digits, and symbols**
- Avoid **common words or names**
- Use **passphrases** like `Bread&Sunshine!EveryDay1`
- Avoid **reusing passwords** across sites
- **Password managers** help remember strong passwords securely

---

## 🛡️ Common Attacks & Prevention

- **Brute Force**: Tries all combinations – strong passwords resist this longer.
- **Dictionary Attack**: Tries common words – avoid real names or dictionary words.
- **Credential Stuffing**: Uses leaked credentials – don’t reuse passwords!

---

## ✅ Conclusion

Strong passwords are long, unique, and complex. Tools like Kaspersky, LastPass, and Security.org help evaluate and improve password strength effectively.
